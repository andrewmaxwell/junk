Move View: WASD / Arrows, scroll to zoom.
Simulate: Space or button at top.
Create Beam: Ctrl/Cmd + Drag. Drag ends to move.
Create Wheel: Ctrl/Cmd + Shift + Drag. Drag center to move or edge to resize.
Select: Click or drag over ends of beams or centers of wheels. Hold Shift to add to the selection, or Alt to remove.
Toggle Fixed: Press F to toggle whether or not selected pieces are fixed.
Delete: Backspace
Undo / Redo: Browser's Back / Forward buttons.
Save: Save the URL
