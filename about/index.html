About Me

Hi, I'm Andrew! A Senior Full Stack Software Engineer at Ocelot Consulting since 2022, I'm passionate about programming, technology, science, math, and more. Alongside my professional endeavors, I live in St. Louis, Missouri, with my wife Andrea, a talented freelance graphic designer, and our two dogs: Telulah, the poodle mix with a grumpy yet endearing personality, and Lulu, the cuddly Yorkie who's always on the lookout. My journey is one of constant learning and exploration, delving into topics like lambda calculus, JavaScript, Lisp, and the fascinating world of various equipment from audio to drones. Outside of work, I serve as a youth leader and the tech guy for Chatham Bible Church, contributing to my community and fostering growth.
