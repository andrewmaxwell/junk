Disclaimer: This is a synthetic browser-based benchmark that measures peak Fused Multiply-Add (FMA) floating-point throughput entirely within CPU registers.

Unlike traditional native benchmarks (e.g., Cinebench, Geekbench), the CPU/GPU tests do not test complex branching or sustained thermal throttling, mapping directly to your browser's JIT Compiler engine efficiency. The System RAM test directly exhausts physical memory bandwidth.
Tips for Maximum Accuracy
  • Plug in your device: Laptops and phones aggressively throttle CPU cores on battery power.
  • Keep the tab focused: Browsers intentionally sabotage execution speed for hidden background tabs to save power.
  • Close heavy apps: Video streams and background tabs will steal essential threads from the Multi-Core test.
  • Thermal Throttling: Running tests repeatedly on a fanless phone/laptop permanently throttles the processor until it cools down. Your first cold run is your fastest!
