This app does billions of calculations per second to simulate the paths of light rays through a simple scene. Over time, it produces an increasingly photorealistic scene created purely by math.

It uses no GPU or hardware acceleration. When the page loads, each CPU core is given instructions on how to render single, low-quality approximations of the scene and send them to the screen as fast as they can, over and over. Over time, they add up to a higher quality image.
