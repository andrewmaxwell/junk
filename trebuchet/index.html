• simSpeed: Increase this to speed up the simulation. Does not affect results.
  • simLength: The number of iterations to run each simulation.
  • popSize: The number of trebuchets in each generation.
  • mutationRate: The probability that any trait will be randomized instead of being calculated as a mix of its parents.
  • breederRatio: If the ratio is 0.2, then the top 20% get to reproduce to make the next generation. Reproduction is always between two different breeders.
  • simsToDisplay: Choose how many simulations to display at once.
  • optimizeFor: Choose the measure of success.
